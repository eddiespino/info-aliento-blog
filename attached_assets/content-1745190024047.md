### Hive Witnesses (aka _"Block Producers"_)

General  Params

- Hive Witnesses operate "consensus" servers (nodes) and ensure the correct behavior of the blockchain.
- They also make consensus decisions for the future development of Hive.
- Each account can vote for 30 witnesses and the vote is proportional to the amount of Hive Power controlled by the account.
